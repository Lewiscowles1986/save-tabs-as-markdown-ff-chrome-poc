# Privacy Policy

This extension, "Save Tab URLs", is designed with a "zero-data" philosophy.

## Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browsing history, or the URLs you save.

## Data Processing
All processing of tab titles and URLs happens locally on your machine within the extension's popup. The data is only used to generate the markdown list displayed to you.

## Data Storage
The extension does not store your data. When you click "Save as...", a file is generated locally and downloaded to your computer.

## Third Parties
No data is ever sent to any third-party servers or services.

# User Policy

This extension is built for one purpose: **for you to save your own damn data.**

We believe that your browsing context and the links you find useful belong to you. This tool provides a simple way to export that information into a portable format (Markdown) so you can keep it, organize it, and use it however you see fit, without being locked into a specific browser or service.
